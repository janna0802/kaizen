What is Kaizen?

Kaizen is a compound of two Japanese words that together translate as "good change" or "improvement." However, Kaizen has come to mean "continuous improvement" through its association with lean methodology and principles. Kaizen Flower Shop took it words from the word itself where the goal is to serve perfection and deliver great quality of flowers. It aims to arrange flowers with meaning and continuously improve the way we cater to customers.
